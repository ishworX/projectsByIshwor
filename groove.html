Groove

A fun simple-to-use discord music bot that focuses on high-quality music and is easy to access. Groove also provides lag-free streaming. You don’t have to worry about bad music quality. You can play music with a link from YouTube, Spotify, Mixer and many other major music streaming platforms. You just have to search for a song by typing its name after the play command. The bot also provides you with the option to create song queues, loop songs, shuffle your cube, see the song lyrics, and many other music adjustments with commands.

Command List

Play

Queue a track or playlist from a search term or url. Supports Spotify, Deezer, Tidal, Apple Music, Soundcloud and more.
Usage:

/play Runaway

!play https://spotify.com/track
